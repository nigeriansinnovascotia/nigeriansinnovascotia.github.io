Become A Member

Thank you for your interest in the Association of Nigerians in Nova Scotia.
Membership is open to all Nigerians living in Nova Scotia, irrespective of ethnic/religious affiliation or legal/residency status in Canada. Simply put, if you are a Nigerian and you live in Nova Scotia, you're eligible to be a member of ANNS.

We preserve and promote the socio-economic, cultural and civic interests of all Nigerians living in Nova Scotia. To achieve this, we organize several events and activities throughout the year to educate and inform our members and also create platforms for positive and meaningful connections with the wider Canadian society.
